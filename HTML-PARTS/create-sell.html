A GUIDE ON CREATING & SELLING NFT’s

Embark on a journey that begins with unleashing your creativity. Learn how to conceptualize.

  1. Educated Yourself

    Before you start, it's crucial to understand what NFTs are, how they work, and the platforms that support them.

  2. Create a Digital Wallet

    To interact with NFTs, you'll need a digital wallet that supports the blockchain your chosen platform uses (commonly Ethereum for most NFTs).

  3. Purchase Crypto

    Acquire cryptocurrency (such as Ethereum) to fund your transactions and cover gas fees. You can purchase cryptocurrency on exchanges.

  4. Choose Marketplace

    Select a reputable NFT marketplace to list and sell your creations. Some popular platforms include OpenSea, Rarible, Mintable, and Foundation.

  5. Mint Your NFT’s

    Minting is the process of turning your digital file into an NFT. This typically involves creating a smart contract that includes details about your work.
